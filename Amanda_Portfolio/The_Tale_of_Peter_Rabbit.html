Peter Rabbit Operetta

 
 
 

All second graders at Prairie View Elementary read the classic story The Tale of Peter Rabbit by Beatrix Potter during the spring semester.  Concurrently, in music class the students learn about how stories are transformed into musicals and operettas.  After much study and practice in both music class and the students’ core classroom, each 2nd grade class presents their own version of the operetta “Peter Rabbit”, music by  in the Prairie View Little Theater.
