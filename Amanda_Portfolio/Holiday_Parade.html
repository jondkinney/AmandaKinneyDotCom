“Holiday Parade”

 
 
 

Prairie View Elementary students in grades K-4 performed “Holiday Parade” on December 13, 2007 for large audiences of parents, staff, and community members.  During the program, students brought the audience on a journey through the PVE holiday parade.  Songs and narrations helped the audience learn about the celebrations of: Christmas, Kwanzaa, Hanukkah, Las Posadas, and winter fun!
