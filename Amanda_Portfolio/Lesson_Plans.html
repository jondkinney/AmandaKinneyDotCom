Sample Lesson Plans


First Grade Lesson Plan

  1. • A first grade lesson that presents ta (quarter note) and ti-ti (two eighth notes) using visual, aural, and kinesthetic means.


Third Grade Lesson Plan

  1. • A third grade lesson that prepares the solfege syllable Re using singing, games, and the use of Orff instruments.


Men's Choir Lesson Plan

  1. • A men’s choir lesson that introduces the style of the spiritual, explores phrasing in relation to text, and challenges students to make a personal connection to their music.


Concert Choir Lesson Plan

  1. • A concert choir lesson that challenges students to find which vocal lines should be brought out amongst the others, teaches the 2/4 conducting pattern, explores a composer’s use of key changes for dramatic effect, and discusses proper singing technique for vowels.
