“The Unity Tree”

 
 
 

Prairie View Elementary students in grades K-4 performed “The Unity Tree” on December 18, 2008 for large audiences of parents, staff, and community members.  During the program, students shared with the audience the story of a village of feuding cats and dogs who learned through their experiences that living and working together in harmony, helping each other through adversity, and sharing ideas and solving problems was an attractive alternative to fighting.
