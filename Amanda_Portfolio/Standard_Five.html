Standard Five:

The teacher uses an understanding of individual and group motivation and behavior to create a learning environment that encourages positive social interaction, active engagement in learning, and self-motivation.




Artifacts:  Lesson Plan, Grade 2, February 19 - 26, 2008

                         Developed while teaching at Prairie View Elementary School

                     Observation Notes written by Laura Brudos, OSD New Teacher Mentor

                                Written during an observation while teaching at Prairie View Elementary, October 2007

               

Knowledge of Individual and Group Motivation:  The lesson plan that I designed for my second grade students at Prairie View Elementary demonstrates my ability to plan lessons that are engaging and motivating for students.  Throughout the class period, the students are challenged through fun music games and activities.  Learning in my classroom is presented as fun yet challenging, and sometimes the students don’t even realize that they are developing new knowledge and skills.  Throughout my lessons I strive to create as many opportunities for intrinsic motivation as possible, as well as help the students develop a life-long understanding and appreciation for music.


Knowledge of Learning Environment: The observation notes written by Laura Brudos, the Oregon School District new teacher mentor, demonstrate my ability to create a positive learning environment in the classroom.  In the notes, she mentions that I greet the students with smiles and hellos at the door and use good praise when responding to student answers.  She also comments that I use “great expression when they (the students) got it wrong.”  Through my interactions with students I strive to create consistent standards of behavior and establish a positive rapport with all.  I always try to create a positive environment that fosters active engagement in learning, self-motivation, and perseverance.  I believe a classroom should be a safe, relaxed, and open learning environment where all students feel welcome and are able to participate freely in the learning process.
